Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Bodygraph ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Bodygraph iOS application.

Information We Collect

Bodygraph stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Weight entries - Daily weight values and optional notes you enter
  • Progress photos - Photos you take or select, stored in the app's private Documents folder
  • App preferences - Unit preference (kg/lbs), notification settings, goal weight

How Your Data Is Stored

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your weight entries, photos, and preferences never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share them (e.g., using the share feature for photo comparisons).

Photos are saved as JPEG files in the app's sandboxed Documents directory. They are not accessible to other apps or uploaded to any server.

Third-Party Services

Bodygraph uses the following third-party service:

  • RevenueCat - For managing in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data and device identifiers as described in their privacy policy. No personal health data (weight, photos) is shared with RevenueCat.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not share or sell any data to third parties

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device using Apple's notification framework. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings within the app. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data, including weight entries and photos.

Children's Privacy

Bodygraph is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@bodygraph.app.
