Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your device Settings, tap your name at the top, then go to Subscriptions. Find Bodygraph and tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Bodygraph, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

Where are my photos stored?

All photos are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. They are not uploaded to any server.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Delete All Data". This will permanently remove all weight entries and photos. You can also uninstall the app to remove everything.

Can I switch between kg and lbs?

Yes! Go to Settings and change your unit preference. All your existing data will be displayed in the new unit automatically.
