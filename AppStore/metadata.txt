Track your weight. Snap your progress. See the difference.

Bodygraph is the simplest way to follow your body transformation journey. Log your daily weight, take progress photos, and compare them side by side to see how far you've come.

YOUR DAILY WEIGHT, BEAUTIFULLY CHARTED
Tap once to log today's weight. Watch your trend unfold on a clean, interactive chart. Switch between 7-day, 30-day, and all-time views.

BEFORE & AFTER — SIDE BY SIDE
Take Front, Side, and Back photos. Then slide the divider to reveal your transformation. This is the moment that keeps you going.

COMPARE ANY TWO PHOTOS
Pick any two dates, same pose, and see them together full-screen. Share your progress with one tap.

STAY CONSISTENT
Set a daily reminder. Track your streak. Small habits lead to big changes.
